Who uses elevation training?

Elevation training is for anyone from professional athletes to the fitness enthusiast looking for the edge. Elevation training is commonly used in running, cycling, swimming, rowing, climbing, speed skating, triathlons, mixed martial arts, skiing, soccer, football, and hockey.
